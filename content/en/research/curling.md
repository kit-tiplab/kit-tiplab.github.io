We aim to establish "curling informatics" as a field, developing next-generation tools for competitive curling strategy and athlete development. Kitami is the curling capital of Japan, giving us unique access to competitive environments and partnerships.

## Digital Scorebook iCE

We develop iCE, a digital scorebook system for detailed recording and analysis of curling game data. It is used in competitive settings and contributes to building a large-scale curling game database.

## AI Tactical Simulation

Using accumulated game data, we develop AI-based tactical simulation systems for strategic decision-making in curling.

## Infrared Stone Tracking System

We develop infrared sensor-based systems for tracking the trajectory of sliding curling stones with high precision.

## Biomechanical Analysis and Curling Robot

We analyze athlete delivery motions from a biomechanical perspective and conduct experimental research using a curling robot.
