We work on establishing methods to define and quantify learner motivation based on the ARCS motivational model (Attention, Relevance, Confidence, Satisfaction).

## Automated Analysis of Course Questionnaires Based on the ARCS Model

We apply text mining techniques to automatically analyze student course evaluation responses, mapping them to the four ARCS categories to provide actionable feedback for course improvement.
