We apply information technology to tourism in order to improve the dissemination and analysis of tourism information and contribute to regional revitalization.

## Information-scientific Content Tourism

We analyze the relationship between media content (anime, film, literature) and regional tourism using computational methods to identify effective strategies for content-driven tourism promotion.

## Okhotsk Tourism Big Data Platform

We are building a platform for collecting and analyzing tourism data from the Okhotsk region of Hokkaido, working with local tourism operators and municipalities to support data-driven tourism strategy.
