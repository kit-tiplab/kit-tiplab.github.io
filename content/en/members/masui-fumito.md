## About Me

Professor at the Faculty of Engineering, Kitami Institute of Technology, and Director of the Winter Sports Science Research Promotion Center. His research interests include tourism informatics, curling informatics, and natural language processing topics such as machine translation, information extraction, and question answering.

**Degrees:** Bachelor (Okayama University); Ph.D (Hokkaido University)
